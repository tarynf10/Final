The Cost of Campaigning

Every presidential election, candidates spend an enormous amount of money. To understand the amount of money candidates spend on campaign-related expenses, let's compare the last two elections with American luxuries that we know to be pricey. This will provide context as to how much money is really being spent on items that may or may not be crucial to the candidate’s campaign, or just make you wonder why John McCain's phone bill is so damn high. At the end, you'll see what you could’ve bought using the total spent. What's more American than spending money on things we don't need? Let’s start!
